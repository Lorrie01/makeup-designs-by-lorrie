About Lorrie

Lorrie has worked in beauty and style for 30+ years. Her makeup artistry has allowed her many opportunities to work on many interesting and creative projects in print, commercial, live performance, corporate, special events and weddings.

Lorrie's expertise in custom blending and creating beautiful flawless faces continues to create a demand for her services.

She has represented many beauty brands for in-store promotions and events including: Chanel, Dior, Smashbox, Revlon, Lorac, Josie Maran, Lancome, Shiseido, Flori Roberts, Fashion Fair, Iman, Visage Beaute, Elizabeth Arden and Almay.
